Capabilities

Core competencies

The seven systems we build.

Each one is a working part of the pipeline, delivered with what it produces and what it changes for the business.

  1. 01

    GTM Strategy

    Turns market priorities into a clear prospecting motion.

    Deliverables

    ICP logic, market maps, workflow roadmap

    Business Outcome

    A focused motion the team can execute and improve.

  2. 02

    Prospecting Systems

    Builds workflows that identify, enrich, qualify, and route target accounts.

    Deliverables

    Sourcing logic, research fields, routing rules

    Business Outcome

    A repeatable source of qualified accounts and contacts.

  3. 03

    Data Enrichment & CRM Hygiene

    Cleans, enriches, and refreshes account and contact data.

    Deliverables

    Field maps, validation rules, refresh logic

    Business Outcome

    Better targeting, cleaner handoffs, and stronger reporting.

  4. 04

    Multi-Channel Outbound

    Connects research, messaging, sequencing, and human follow-up.

    Deliverables

    Message architecture, sequence logic, trigger rules

    Business Outcome

    Consistent outreach that reaches the right accounts at the right time.

  5. 05

    Pipeline Operations

    Defines ownership, routing, QA, and reporting for pipeline workflows.

    Deliverables

    CRM rules, dashboards, QA checks

    Business Outcome

    Clear handoffs and visibility across the funnel.

  6. 06

    Warm Contact Intelligence

    Maps relationship paths and warm introductions into target accounts.

    Deliverables

    Relationship maps, contact paths, activation workflows

    Business Outcome

    More effective outreach through trusted connections.

  7. 07

    Data Warehouse Enrichment

    Enriches internal datasets with structured fields and quality checks.

    Deliverables

    Enriched schema, quality report, operating notes

    Business Outcome

    Data that can support actual GTM decisions.

Delivery model

Each engagement moves from operating question to working system.

  1. 01

    Diagnose

    We review the current motion, stack, data quality, manual work, and commercial priorities, so the build targets real constraints, not assumptions.

    Stack & data audit · Manual-work map · Commercial priorities
  2. 02

    Design

    Target workflow, business rules, data model, ownership, and success measures, documented so everyone agrees on what good looks like.

    Target workflow · Business rules & data model · Ownership & metrics
  3. 03

    Implement

    We build, connect, test, and document the system inside your stack, so the motion runs where the work happens, not in a slide.

    Built & connected · Tested end-to-end · Documented
  4. 04

    Transfer

    We train owners, set up QA and monitoring, and leave an iteration backlog, so the motion keeps improving after we step out.

    Owner training · QA & monitoring · Iteration backlog
